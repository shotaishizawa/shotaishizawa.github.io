Beverly Hacks

BlueBomb was involved in organizing Beverly Hills High School’s first hackathon. Hackathons provide a venue for self-expression and creativity through technology. The hackers, regardless of their background and experience, come together, form teams around a problem or idea, and collaboratively code a unique solution from scratch — these generally take shape in the form of websites, mobile apps, virtual reality games, drones and robots. If a participant is a beginner in programming, various workshops, mentors, and even other skilled hackers in his or her age group are available for them to learn and receive help during the event.
